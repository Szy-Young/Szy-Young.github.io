Ziyang Song

I am currently a 2nd-year PhD student at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, supervised by Prof. Bo Yang.

Previously, I obtained my M.Eng and B.Eng degrees (Honors Youth Program) from Xi'an Jiaotong University, supervised by Prof. Zejian Yuan. During my M.Eng study, I interned at SenseTime with Dongliang Wang, and Tencent Robotics X with Wanchao Chi.

My research interests lie in computer vision and machine learning. Currently I am focusing on unsupervised methods for 3D segmentation and reconstruction.
